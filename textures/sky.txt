The demo expects a sky box image named sky.png here.
The image should have a 4:3 width:height ratio.
The expected layout of the sky box is as follows:
 U
LFRB
 D
U: Up, D: Down, L: Left, R: Right, F: Front, B: Back

One possible sky box which looks good can be found here (as of 20-Feb-2016):
http://mi.eng.cam.ac.uk/~agk34/resources/textures/sky/sky.png
